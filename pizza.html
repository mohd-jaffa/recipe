Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni or other toppings (e.g., mushrooms, bell peppers, onions)
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • Olive oil for brushing

Instructions:

  • Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C) or the temperature recommended for your pizza dough (10 minutes).
  • Prepare Dough: Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness. Transfer to a pizza stone or baking sheet (5 minutes).
  • Add Sauce: Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges (2 minutes).
  • Add Toppings: Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce. Add your desired toppings and sprinkle with dried oregano and basil (3 minutes).
  • Bake Pizza: Brush the edges of the dough with olive oil. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and melted (15 minutes).
  • Serve: Let the pizza cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving (5 minutes).
