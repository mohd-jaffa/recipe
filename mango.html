Mango shake

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe mango, peeled and cubed
  • 1 cup vanilla or plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk (or more, depending on desired consistency)
  • 2 tbsp honey or sugar (optional, adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions:

  • Blend Ingredients: In a blender, combine mango cubes, yogurt, milk, honey (or sugar), and vanilla extract if using. Add a few ice cubes if you prefer a colder shake (5 minutes).
  • Blend Until Smooth: Blend until smooth and creamy. Adjust sweetness and consistency by adding more honey or milk if needed (2 minutes).
  • Serve: Pour into glasses and serve immediately (1 minute).
