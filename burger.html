Burger

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef (80% lean)
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 burger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • 4 slices of cheese (cheddar, Swiss, etc.)
  • Tomato slices
  • Pickles (optional)
  • Ketchup and mustard (optional)

Instructions:

  • Prepare Patties: In a bowl, mix ground beef with egg, breadcrumbs, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Form into 4 patties (about 5 minutes).
  • Cook Patties: Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 160°F (71°C) for medium doneness. Add a slice of cheese on each patty in the last minute of cooking and cover to melt (10 minutes).
  • Toast Buns: While the patties cook, toast the burger buns on a grill or in a toaster (2-3 minutes).
  • Assemble Burgers: Place the cooked patties on the bottom halves of the buns. Top with lettuce, tomato slices, pickles, and condiments if desired. Cover with the top halves of the buns (2 minutes).
  • Serve: Serve immediately with your favorite side dishes.
